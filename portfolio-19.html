Case Study
for eCycle App

Date 2022.04 - 2022.05
Project Role Case Study / Prototype
Purpose Redesign / Proposal
Form Mobile App

The Canadian-born company, eCycle is an e-bike-sharing system that provides Electric bikes to people to use within various city locations situated across Canada.

Up until now, eCycle users have only been able to locate available bikes and rent from the station kiosk. For this reason, I created the case study and designed this mobile app based on the project brief.

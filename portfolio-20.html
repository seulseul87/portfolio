Game of Thrones
Advanced Icons

Date 2021.02 - 2021.03
Project Role Ideation / Design / Illustration
Purpose Creating Advanced Icons
Form Icon Brand Book

Game of Thrones is an American fantasy drama television series created by David Benioff and D. B. Weiss for HBO. It is an adaptation of A Song of Ice and Fire, a series of fantasy novels by George R. R. Martin.

In this icon brand book, I chose 6 prominent characters of the series to create a special icon set keeping consistency. These icons have similarities such as facial structure, geometric style, and round shape of the outline.

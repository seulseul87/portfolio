Traditional Food
Cookbook

Date 2010.03 - 2010.05
Role Editorial Layout Design
Project Purpose Publication
Conformation Book

This book summarizes 3,252 local foods from nine regions of Korea. It is planned to enhance the value of traditional local food. A total of three books were produced.

The first is food made from the mountains, fields and the sea. The second is good food to serve when inviting guests. The third is classified as healthy food.
